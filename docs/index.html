Frequently Asked Questions

What is PianoMaker (Piano Maker)?

PianoMaker (also written as Piano Maker) is a free AI-powered piano learning platform that transforms any song into interactive Piano Tiles. It combines music transcription with structured learning exercises, fingering guides, and visual learning tools to help you master piano efficiently.

Is Piano Maker really free?

Yes! PianoMaker is completely free with unlimited song transcriptions, full access to Piano Tiles player, all learning exercises, and export features. No credit card required, no subscription, no hidden fees. We believe quality music education should be accessible to everyone.

What is Piano Tiles and how does it work?

Piano Tiles is an interactive visual learning method where notes cascade down the screen in real-time, showing you exactly when and which keys to press. It's like Guitar Hero for piano – making learning intuitive and fun, especially for beginners who find traditional sheet music challenging.

Can I import songs from TikTok and Instagram?

Absolutely! Just copy the link from TikTok, Instagram, or SoundCloud and paste it into PianoMaker. We'll extract the audio, transcribe it, and create interactive Piano Tiles for you. Perfect for learning trending songs and viral piano covers instantly.

What instruments does Piano Maker support?

While PianoMaker is optimized for piano and keyboard learning, our AI can transcribe music from any instrument in the recording. The Piano Tiles and learning exercises are specifically designed for piano players.

Do I need to download an app?

No! PianoMaker is entirely web-based and works perfectly in any browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile. No app store, no downloads, no installation – just open your browser and start learning immediately.

What are the structured learning exercises?

Each song includes a complete learning curriculum: warm-up exercises to prepare your hands, fingering guides showing which fingers to use, technique drills for specific challenges, and progressive difficulty levels. It's designed like real piano lessons.

Can I export sheet music and MIDI files?

Yes! You can export your transcriptions as PDF sheet music, MIDI files (quantized & unquantized), and MusicXML format. These work with all major music notation software like MuseScore, Finale, and Sibelius.

How accurate is the AI transcription?

Our AI delivers accurate transcriptions for clean recordings. While no automated system is perfect, the interactive Piano Tiles player and structured exercises help you learn effectively regardless. Plus, our AI is constantly improving with regular updates.

Is PianoMaker good for beginners?

Absolutely! The Piano Tiles visual learning method is perfect for beginners who find traditional sheet music intimidating. Combined with fingering guides, warm-ups, and progressive difficulty, it's designed to take you from zero to playing songs you love.

Can I use Piano Maker offline?

PianoMaker requires an internet connection to transcribe songs and access the learning platform. However, once you've transcribed a song, you can export it as PDF or MIDI to practice offline.

How do I get started with Piano Maker?

Simply visit pianomaker.art, import a song (upload a file or paste a link from TikTok/Instagram/SoundCloud), and our AI will create interactive Piano Tiles and exercises for you. No signup required to try it – start learning in 30 seconds!
